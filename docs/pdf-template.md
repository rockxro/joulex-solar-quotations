# PDF Template

PDF generation uses `@react-pdf/renderer`.

The first template is intentionally fixed and simple. The goal is to produce a professional quotation document before investing in final branding.

---

## Sections

1. Header with installer company data.
2. Client data.
3. System type and installed capacity.
4. Monthly solar result summary.
5. Product lines with quantities and totals.
6. Commercial notes.
7. Final price and signature/footer area.

---

## Rules

- Use CLP formatting with no decimals.
- Use the saved quotation snapshot as source data.
- Do not depend on screenshots or DOM rendering.
- Keep final branding deferred until the business flow is stable.

---

## Future Branding Inputs

- Installer company logo.
- Corporate colors.
- Legal/commercial footer.
